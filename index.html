FAQ

How long does a full interior detail take?

Typically 3–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

Do you prep vehicles for PCS shipping?

Yes. Our PCS Shipping Package follows the common carrier guidelines for Guam.

Is ceramic coating worth it in Guam’s climate?

Yes—ceramic helps resist salt air, UV, and frequent rain. We use System-X products.

How much is a headlight restoration?

Prices vary by vehicle; ask for the Shine & Protect bundle to save when paired with wax.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Walk-ins welcome Mon–Sat 8:30–4:30, but appointments ensure the fastest turnaround.
